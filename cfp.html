The 7th International Conference on Informatics and Applied Mathematics IAM'24
November 27-28, 2024 GUELMA, ALGERIA

 

Call for Papers

Call for Papers

The conference program will include Oral presentations, poster sessions and demonstrations, alongside with keynote speakers and industrial workshops.

Submission Guidelines

All submissions must be written in English, original, unpublished, and not considered elsewhere for publication. The initial submission for review will be limited to 20 pages, including references, figures and tables and formatted according to the conference template. Articles should be carefully checked and proofread before submission.

Review Process

All paper submissions will be double-blind peer reviewed by 2-3 experts in the field based on originality, technical quality, presentation, research content and the relevance to conference topics.

Submission Process

Authors are kindly invited to submit their papers/ posters/ demo in PDF, docx or doc format, using the conference template:( Download MS Word Paper Format) OR in Latex format ( Download Latex Format) , via the submission system at the address: https://cmt3.research.microsoft.com/IAM2024/Submission/Index
